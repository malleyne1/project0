Food I Eat

What you eat will render the results of how you come out of quarantine. Therefore, it is important that you are having well-balanced meals.

Three main meals:

Breakfast

According to WebMd.com Breakfast kick-starts your metabolism, helping you burn calories throughout the day. It also gives you the energy you need to get things done and helps you focus at work or at school. Those are just a few reasons why it’s the most important meal of the day. Studies have linked eating breakfast to:

  • Good health
  • Better memory and concentration
  • Lower levels of “bad” LDL cholesterol
  • Lower chances of getting diabetes, heart disease, and being overweight

Lunch

Food is what gives you energy. Lunch raises your blood sugar level in the middle of the day, making you be able to focus for the rest of the afternoon.

Dinner

Dinner is important to provide energy and nutrients for the needs of the body. Regular dietary intake is important to ensure the body gets the energy and nutrients needed to function normally
