Exercise Routines

Being home can make one feel relaxed, so relaxed to the point where one doesn't want to do anything besides binge waiting tv shows and eating comfort food. Well, I couldn't just allow myself to just do nothing. So, I decided to start an exercise challenge. I thought an exercise challenge would give me the motivation and the accountability to get my butt moving on a daily basis.

There are so many different exercises out there that does so many different things. It's all based on your personal goals. Mine is weightloss and here are a few I like to use. Variety is the key for me because the same routine becomes boring. We all know what happens when we're bored with something...we stop.
