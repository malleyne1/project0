The Way I Relax

At this time of quarantine it is important that we stay calm. Relaxation is not only about peace of mind but is a process that reduces the effects of stress on the mind and body. It helps us battle the stress of everyday life responsibilities and the lack of providing those nessecities. Stress has a tremendous effect on your health; therefore, it is in your best interest to be safe and relax.
